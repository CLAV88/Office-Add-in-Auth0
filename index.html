WELCOME
This add-in demonstrates how to use the Auth0 service to simplify social login.

To use this add-in

  • 1 Click the Sign In button
  • 2 On the popup that opens, click a button for one of the identity services.
  • 3 If you are not already logged into the service, the service's sign in form opens in the popup.
  • 4 After you login, the popup closes and the main working page of the add-in opens in the task pane. Click the button to insert your name into the document.
